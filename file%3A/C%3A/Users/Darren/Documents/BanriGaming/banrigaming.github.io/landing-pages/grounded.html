Apex Predator
Notes

This Reaper build is a glass cannon powerhouse built for overwhelming offense and relentless speed. With a base 21% critical hit chance that stacks infinitely thanks to the Mantis Trinket (+2% crit per 3-hit combo), you’ll be critting almost constantly. Combined with +5% from Coup de Grass, +5% from Perfect Toast, +5% from Sticky Boss Sauce, and +60% crit damage from Trapper Peeper, your burst potential skyrockets. The Scythe of Blossoms (Mighty) adds dual damage typing and an extra +5% crit chance, while Apex Predator grants +25% attack speed after unlocking it from defeating the Mantis. Pair this with Death’s Impetus from Widow armor for +15% attack speed and 150 HP healing over 10 seconds after each kill, pushing total speed to a game-breaking +40%. Crit effects include Crit Stun (25 stun damage), Cutman (200 bleed over 10s), and Acid Splash (15% enemy damage vulnerability). With additional boosts from Liquid Rage (+10% damage) and Human Food (+10% resistance), plus your Fire Ant pet (+25% total damage), this setup becomes a death machine. Perfect for bosses like the Mantis, Wasp Queen, and Broodmother, this build excels in both single-target burst and crowd-clearing efficiency—leaving a trail of bodies and crit pop-ups in your wake.
